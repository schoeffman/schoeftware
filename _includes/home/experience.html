Capstone

Capstone was an action puzzle game I built by myself. You can check out a video of the gameplay here The game was built from the ground up with every vertex constructed by hand. I also ran all the physics through my custom game engine. Levels were templated out though xml and read in to allow for quick and easy level construction. A really fun project that I am extremely proud of.
